Cheeseburger

A cheeseburger is a hamburger topped with cheese. Traditionally, the slice of cheese is placed
on top of the meat patty. The cheese is usually added to the hamburger patty near the end
of the cooking time, which allows the cheese to melt.

Ingredients

  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Onions
  • Slice
  • Beef patty

Steps

  1. Handling your meat properly
  2. Cheese them babies and toast those buns
  3. A rested burger is a juicy burger
  4. Letture should not be a topping
  5. Crown your burgers with bacon
