Coffee

Coffee is a beverage prepared from roasted coffee beans. Darkly colored, bitter, and slightly acidic,
coffee has a stimulating effect on humans, primarily due to its caffeine content. It has the highest
sales in the world market for hot drinks.

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Place coffee in your espresso maker
  2. Add the water
  3. Heat the milk
  4. After heating pour the coffee the add the milk
